1. Executive Summary

GOO is an asset-backed stable utility token designed to create a stable and usable unit of value within exchange networks and payment services.

GOO is issued centrally by the publisher but implements mechanisms for reserve transparency, redemption, and transparent management behavior. The token combines USD backing with physical gold reserves to maintain long-term value stability.

Key Features:

  • Asset-Backed: 75% USD reserves + 25% physical gold
  • Utility Token: Designed for exchange cashback and ecosystem use
  • Transparent: Regular attestations and reserve reporting
  • Compliant: KYC/AML integration and regulatory compliance
  • Redeemable: Users can redeem tokens for USD equivalent

Short-term Goals:

  • Develop MVP token and presale system
  • Accept USDT/USDC/ETH/WBTC and fiat through PSP
  • Integrate with partner exchanges for cashback programs

Long-term Vision:

  • Create comprehensive payment ecosystem
  • Enable swaps and DEX/CEX listings
  • Expand utility across goods and services

2. Vision & Value Proposition

For Exchange Users:

  • Receive cashback rewards in GOO tokens instead of cash discounts
  • Hold and use tokens within the ecosystem or redeem near USD value
  • Benefit from stable value preservation with gold backing

For Exchanges/Partners:

  • Customer acquisition tool and reduced marketing costs
  • Allocate portion of trading fees to GOO distribution
  • Enhanced user engagement and retention

For Investors:

  • Token with structured backing and economic model
  • Focus on preserving purchasing power (USD + gold reserve)
  • Transparent reserve management and reporting
Competitive Advantage: Combination of cash and physical gold reserves reduces currency and inflation risks while providing transparency through attestations and periodic reports.

7. Operational Flow

Presale Process:

  1. User completes KYC and gets whitelisted
  2. User sends payment (USDC/USDT/ETH/fiat) to presale contract
  3. For fiat payments: Off-chain confirmation by team
  4. Backend requests mint to GOO contract
  5. Tokens allocated to buyer's wallet
  6. Upon softCap achievement: Funds moved to multisig treasury

Redemption Process:

  1. User requests redemption (KYC still required)
  2. System burns tokens and calculates USD equivalent
  3. USD (or fiat equivalent) transferred to user's bank/payment address
  4. Daily redemption caps and fees apply
  5. Transparent reporting of all redemptions
Policy: Daily redemption caps and fees will be clearly defined with transparent reporting.
